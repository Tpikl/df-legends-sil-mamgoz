### Brief Summary
The Unswerving Fells is the sole elf civilization in Sil Mamgoz. Founded in three forest retreats, it has been completely destroyed over 500 years of devastating losses. All 19 *historical figures* (notable individuals) have perished - the last being Caraca Flutebeaks in Year 36. All three forest retreats now stand abandoned. The 20 surviving elves exist as a scattered, homeless population without leaders, territory, or organized government. Like the dwarves and humans, elven civilization in Sil MÃ¥mgoz has been marked as "Lost."

## Religion & Culture

### Pantheon Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Deity Count** | 1 |
| **Primary Faith** | Monotheistic |
| **Deity** | Famime (#73) |
| **Divine Spheres** | Nature, Rivers |

The elves practiced a pure, nature-focused monotheism. Their single deity Famime embodies the natural world - forests, rivers, and the wild. This stands in stark contrast to the humans' ten-god pantheon with its dark spheres of deformity and sacrifice.

*Full pantheon details: [elf_pantheon_the_unswerving_fells.md](elf_pantheon_the_unswerving_fells.md)*

### Cultural Notes
- Forest retreats centered around nature - markets, not mead halls
- Many elves had goals to "see the great natural sites" - pilgrimage culture
- Journey pets were common: leopards, crocodiles, giant cougars, giant ticks
- Master skills: herbalism, bowyer, weaving, woodcraft, clothesmaking, animal care
- Social skills highly valued: conversation, flattery, comedy, negotiation
- Some elves engaged in counterintelligence plots (Rifafa, Sareve, Ova Guildswamps, Kifino)

## Timeline of Major Events

### Year 1 - Foundation
- Soundedglacier, Songpraised, and Wavebalds founded
- Rofa Fancywaxes becomes Druid
- Rifafa Creaturebrim becomes Princess
- Enure Gravelincenses becomes Diplomat
- First battles at settlements begin

### Year 2 - The First Catastrophe
- Human civilization attacks Songpraised
- **3 elves killed**: Rifafa Creaturebrim, Enure Gravelincenses, Pila Blazedbored
- Eletha Duskboarded born to Sareve (first recorded elf birth)

### Years 3-4 - Rebuilding
- Sareve becomes Princess, Caraca becomes Diplomat, Afe becomes Acolyte
- Ule Silkyobey born (Year 3)
- Fecici Thornwaves born (Year 4)
- Titan Umbrallilac wounds Oda Touchmeadows (Year 4)

### Year 5 - Songpraised Falls
- **Titan Umbrallilac destroys Songpraised**
- Oda Touchmeadows killed
- The Unswerving Fells loses its first settlement

### Year 7 - Reclamation
- Elves reclaim Songpraised under new site government (#41)
- Eletha Duskboarded and Vema Toefin settle there

### Years 13-21 - The Bleeding Years
- Year 13: Eletha Duskboarded killed, Vema Toefin abducted (transformed?)
- Year 15: Thone Jawknow (1 year old) killed by titan, Imepe Roarmauve killed
- Year 19: Irene Tickstaupe killed
- Year 20: Ula Beastrivers killed
- Year 21: Ova Coalcouple and Caraca Boldsnarling killed by night creatures

### Year 25 - The Second Catastrophe
- Human attack on Songpraised
- **3 elves killed in battle**: Sareve Packsoothes, Fecici Thornwaves, Afe Basinsense
- Only 4 documented elves remain

### Years 26-32 - The End Approaches
- Year 26: Ova Guildswamps becomes Princess, killed same year
- Year 27: Kifino Drilledzephyrs becomes Princess
- Year 32: **Titan Umbrallilac attacks Soundedglacier**
  - Rofa Fancywaxes killed (leader for 31 years)
  - Ule Silkyobey killed (Acolyte)
  - Kifino Drilledzephyrs killed (Princess)

### Year 36 - Loss of Leaders
- Caraca Flutebeaks, the last historical figure, killed by Yeti Pima Snarlblot
- **All notable elf leaders now dead** - but general population endures
