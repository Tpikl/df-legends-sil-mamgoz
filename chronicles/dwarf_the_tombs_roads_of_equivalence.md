### Brief Summary
The Tombs-Roads of Equivalence was the sole dwarven civilization in Sil Mamgoz. Once a proud kingdom of fortresses and hillocks, it has been completely destroyed - not by external enemies, but by civil war. The Crystalline Knights (#52), a rebellious dwarf faction, systematically conquered every settlement between Years 159-501. The original fortress of Longpaints fell in Year 162. The last two sites, founded in Year 500 as a desperate attempt to rebuild, were destroyed and abandoned within a year. Today, 312 dwarves remain as a scattered population without a home, their civilization marked as "Lost" in the annals of history.

**The Fall of Longpaints (Year 162):**
The conquest of the original fortress marked the effective end of the civilization. The Orb Of Heroes (#27), the founding site government, was overthrown. The Crystalline Knights installed their own government (#58).

**The Year 500 Tragedy:**
The Tombs-Roads attempted a desperate comeback, founding two new fortresses (Swordreveres and Violentwhipped). The Crystalline Knights responded immediately - Swordreveres was destroyed the same year it was founded. Violentwhipped was abandoned the following year, marking the final end of the civilization.

### What Happened
The Tombs-Roads of Equivalence suffered a complete collapse:

1. **Early History (Years 1-42):** Promising start, but hillocks repeatedly destroyed by titans, hydras, and the roc Litast
2. **The Civil War (Years 159-162):** The Crystalline Knights conquered all remaining sites, including the capital Longpaints
3. **The Exile (Years 163-499):** Survivors existed as scattered refugees with no home
4. **The Final Attempt (Year 500-501):** Two new fortresses founded in desperation; both lost within a year

### The Crystalline Knights
The true destroyers of the Tombs-Roads were not external enemies, but fellow dwarves. The Crystalline Knights (#52) systematically conquered every settlement and now control the former dwarf territory. The nature of this rebellion - ideological, dynastic, or opportunistic - remains unclear.

### Prospects
The Tombs-Roads of Equivalence is **extinct as a territorial entity**. While 312 dwarves survive in the general population, they have no settlements, no functioning government, and no territory. The Crystalline Knights have proven willing to destroy any attempt to rebuild. Without divine intervention or a dramatic reversal, this civilization will not recover.
