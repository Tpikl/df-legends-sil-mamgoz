## Character Archetypes

- **Oce:** The eternal matriarch. Ancient, patient, methodical. Seeks artistic fulfillment despite her nature.
- **Vema:** The corrupted hunter. Once an elf connected to nature, now uses his archery to kill his former kin.
- **Murkyghost:** The born predator. Never knew mortal life, raised in darkness.
- **Nacu:** The fallen bureaucrat. A papermaker and politician who fell to Oce's influence.
- **Siti:** The devoted craftsman. Master armorsmith who worships fervently, seduced by Murkyghost.
- **Ostar:** The silent stalker. Third generation, pure shadow.
- **Zutosp:** The most efficient killer. Highest combat reflexes, deadliest of the young generation.
